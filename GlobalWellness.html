Empowering Global Wellness with Medyatra

At Medyatra, we are pioneering a transformative approach to medical tourism, delivering unparalleled access to premier healthcare providers and insurance services, both locally and internationally, through our GlobalWellness Program.

Unlocking Boundless Healthcare Opportunities

Our Universal Patient Health Access Identity (UPHAI) card acts as your digital health passport, storing your medical records securely and allowing seamless healthcare experiences anywhere in the world.

  • Single-Card Convenience: Experience the freedom to access premium healthcare services globally, with just one card.
  • Digital Medical Records: Have your medical history at your fingertips, eliminating the hassles of managing extensive paperwork.
  • Universal Accessibility: Whether it’s local or international, connect with multiple healthcare and insurance providers effortlessly.

End-to-End Healthcare Solutions

Medyatra's innovative platform offers a myriad of solutions and services, designed to cater to every aspect of your medical tourism journey, ensuring it is smooth, secure, and exceptionally curated.

  • Comprehensive Assistance: From travel arrangements to medical appointments, we provide meticulous assistance at every step.
  • Global Connectivity: Explore and connect with a diverse network of renowned healthcare providers and insurance firms across the globe.
  • Swift Insurance Processes: Experience seamless and swift insurance services, making your international healthcare journey worry-free.
  • Instant Medical Consultations: Leverage our platform to access instant medical consultations from world-class healthcare providers.

Connecting You to a World of Wellness

Enlist in our GlobalWellness Program and open the doors to a world brimming with elite healthcare services, specialized medical treatments, and integrated insurance solutions, regardless of geographical constraints.

Join the GlobalWellness Program

Discover a realm of medical excellence and innovation with Medyatra. For more information and to start your global wellness journey, Contact Us. Let’s transcend borders and redefine healthcare experiences together!
