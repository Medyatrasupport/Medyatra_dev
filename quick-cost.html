Welcome to Medyatra Estimation Calculator

The Medyatra Estimation Calculator is a sophisticated tool designed to empower healthcare providers by offering insights into operational costs, potential savings, and additional revenue through Medyatra's innovative solutions.

Ready to explore the potential of Medyatra in transforming your healthcare services? Start using the Medyatra Estimation Calculator now and embark on a journey towards operational excellence and enhanced revenue!

Calculated Metrics

Below are the calculated metrics derived from several input metrics to evaluate the financial performance and operational efficiency of a medical setup. These metrics provide insights into revenue, expenses, and operational aspects.

  • License Cost: Calculated as doctorsCount * licenseCostPerYear * licenseYears.
  • Yearly Patient Count: Calculated as patientCount * 365.
  • Yearly Revenue From OPD: Calculated as consultationFee * yearlyPatientCount.
  • Total Employee Cost: Calculated as employeeCount * employeeCost * 12.
  • Pages To Be Given: Equal to yearlyPatientCount.
  • Cartridges Required: Calculated as ceil(yearlyPatientCount / 500).
  • Total Cartridge Cost: Calculated as cartridgesRequired * 5000.
  • Maintenance Cost: Calculated as printerCount * 7000.
  • Paper Cost Yearly: Calculated as perPaperCost * yearlyPatientCount.
  • Software Cost Per Doctor: Calculated as doctorsCount * 18000.
  • Total Expense: Sum of totalEmployeeCost, totalCartridgeCost, maintenanceCost, paperCostYearly, and softwareCostPerDoctor.
  • Expense Ratio: Calculated as (totalExpense / yearlyRevenueFromOPD) * 100.
  • Basic Card Sales: Calculated as 550 * 0.10 * yearlyPatientCount.
  • Medyatra Charge: Calculated as yearlyRevenueFromOPD * 0.10.
  • Revenue Before Medyatra: Calculated as yearlyRevenueFromOPD - totalExpense.
  • Revenue After Medyatra: Calculated as yearlyRevenueFromOPD + basicCardSales + medyatraCharge - totalExpense.
  • Total Increase in Revenue: Calculated as revenueAfterMedyatra - revenueBeforeMedyatra.
  • Percentage Increase: Calculated as (totalIncreaseInRevenue / revenueBeforeMedyatra) * 100.
